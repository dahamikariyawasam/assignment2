About Us

Turtle hatcheries in Sri Lanka play a vital role in protecting endangered sea turtles.

Turtles Lanka - The Turtle Hatcheries Organisation


Things we do

RESCUE

We rescue turtles that have been entangled in fishing gear, injured or stranded.

REHABILITATION

We give them the care and rehabilitation they need to recover

RELEASE

After they recover, they are released back to their natural habitats.
